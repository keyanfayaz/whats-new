# Topic

## Subject

AI tools and the AI industry (new model releases, product launches, notable
research, funding and major business moves) plus general sports news (major
results, trades, signings, and headline storylines across major leagues).

## Audience & goal

Just me, to stay current. The update should let me skim in under two minutes
and know what actually moved in AI and in sports that day — no homework, no
hype, just what changed and why it matters.

## Exclusions

- No stock-price or share-movement chatter unless it reflects a concrete
  business event (acquisition, major funding round, leadership change).
- No unsourced rumors — every item needs at least one credible link.
- Skip minor incremental version bumps with no user-facing impact.
- Headlines already in `updates/_ledger.md`, unless there is a concrete new
  development today (in which case use the same slug and prefix the daily
  bullet with `Update —`).
